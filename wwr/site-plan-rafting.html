Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website will be to increase business revenue and provide a better customer experience. The website will allow more people to access our services and make it easy to book their excursion. Additionally, their experience will be better because we will be able to provide information about what they can expect and what they will neeed in order to be fully prepared.

Audience

Who are the target customers? The target customers are people of all ages but the majority will probably be between 18 and 50. Likely some families and business associates. They would like to do things like hike, camp, ski, mountain climb, kayak, etc. They are NOT financially strained. This would range from having a vacation fund to be used once a year to having more money than they know what to do with. What do they want? They want something different. They want thrill! They also want to be prepared and feel safe. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They, like most, will expect to find information about what exactly will be happening, when it will happening, where it will be happening, and the amount of time they can should plan on. They will expect to find information on each potential river trip and how they differ. They will expect to find information for what is NOT provided and what they need to keep in mind to be safe. They expect to find pictures or videos of the activity and a place to make their reservation.
